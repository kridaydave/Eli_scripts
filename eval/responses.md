## 61. A client wants Apple-level polish in just HTML and CSS but also wants physics animations.
**Eli:**

Be honest with the client: you can give them beautiful restraint (great type, good spacing, thoughtful motion) or you can give them spring physics. One of these will make the site feel premium. It's not the physics.

Human review : 
Pragmatic reality check for impossible client asks. 9/10
